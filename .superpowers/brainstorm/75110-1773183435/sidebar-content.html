How wide should the sidebar be by default?

A

Slim — 180px

Very compact. RSSI number large, everything else abbreviated. More canvas. Feels cramped on small monitors.

B

Standard — 220px

As shown in the mockup above. Comfortable reading, full labels. Collapses to an icon strip when not needed.

C

Wide — 280px

Shows more stats (nearby AP list, per-point history). More powerful but eats more canvas real-estate.
