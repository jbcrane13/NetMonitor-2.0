Pick a Heatmap Color Scheme

Each scheme uses smooth gradient interpolation between measurement points. Click one to select it.

✓
Thermal
Blue → cyan → green → yellow → red. Classic infrared camera look. Visually striking and immediately legible.
✓
Signal (Default)
Red → orange → yellow → green. Direct signal quality mapping. Universally understood traffic-light semantics.
✓
Nebula
Dark navy → violet → magenta → white. Stunning on dark floors plans. Premium, distinctive aesthetic.
✓
Arctic
Deep navy → teal → ice blue → white. Cool, technical. Strong signal burns bright white; dead zones disappear into darkness.

Display Modes (can be combined)

✓
🌡
Gradient Heatmap
Smooth radial interpolation between measurement points. Shows estimated coverage between tapped locations.
✓
⚬
Measurement Dots
Show raw tap locations as labeled circles with dBm values. Good for precision review of recorded data.
✓
〰
Contour Lines
Signal strength iso-lines like a topographic map. Shows coverage boundaries clearly at −50, −65, −80 dBm.
✓
◫
Dead Zone Highlight
Pulses red on areas with weak or no recorded signal. Immediately shows where to move the router.
